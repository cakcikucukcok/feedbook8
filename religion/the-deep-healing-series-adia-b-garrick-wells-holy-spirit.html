The Deep Healing Series is a collection of workbooks designed to discover, process, and heal the broken places in our hearts and in our lives through the application of scripture and deeper understanding of God's heart towards us. Exercises in each workbook take you gently through each subject, and assists in understanding more, digging deeper, and rising through the storm. This Series is for groups and individuals who seek to delve deep to the roots of hard topics in the human experience, and be empowered to move beyond, toward whole living and being through the Word of God. In this series, you will:

- Gain further insights into how scripture can be applied to real life and real experiences

- Learn how God speaks to those areas of deep need through His love for us

- Move systematically through the significant experiences in your life that have impacted your sense of self, peace, or personal contribution to the world and others

- Receive bible based tools for addressing areas and aids for ongoing healing and support

- Experience deeper connection to the triune God through prayer and meditation on relevant scripture

Allow the Holy Spirit and the Word of God to bring you into new life today. Let The Deep Healing Series hold your hand as God holds your heart.

The Confession and Forgiveness Workbook shares more about these two powerful tools, and how significant they are in everyday living and relationships. Confession and Forgiveness takes a closer look at how the two relate to create liberty when practiced regularly.
