The message provided in the Keys to Good Success is a simple one. It reminds us that we are equipped with the resources needed to be successful in all aspects of life. A strong and lasting foundation is required for any construction or building project to be successful. The same foundational premise holds true for the building of a successful life. Each key is a foundational building block in the form of a principle. Good success keys provide durable foundations as we apply them to the building of our lives. Spiritual, emotional, physical, and financial areas are strengthened. Self-esteem, creativity, confidence, and a new way of thinking are enhanced.Supporters of the Keys to Good Success discipline believe and know that they can conquer any challenge that comes their way.
