Sam and the Floorboard Gang, the First Adventure, is a childrens fantasy book that takes you inside a world you never knew existed.

Sam tries it an ends up spending most of his nights in Wee Town flying with his new friend, Bugboy, and having one great adventure after another!

Sam and the Floorboard Gang, the First Adventure, is a must-read for children who like easy, fast-paced, fantasy-adventure books!
