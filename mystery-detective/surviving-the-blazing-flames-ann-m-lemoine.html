One hot summer day, a fire breaks loose in a family home in Summerdale, Alabama, and Ryann, a young first responder following in her firefighter father’s footsteps, responds to the blaze along with many others. But after much heartbreak, everyone realizes the fire was no accident. Someone intentionally lit the inferno. 

Soon, a fire is set every week, almost like clockwork. Someone is out there trying to get the fire department’s attention, trying to tell them a message. The fires always occur during Ryann’s shifts, and she’s sure whoever is setting them is trying to get to her family. Ryann knows it, but how can she prove it?

Leaning heavily on her partner, Ryann must chase down this serial killer arsonist. Will true love help Ryann find who wants her family dead? Or will it be too late?
