Words of Life celebrates love and friendship in verse and shares in the life of someone who has been through life experiences shared by so many. In a world where there is such uncertainty and chaos, the emotions expressed in these poems appear in tranquil and peaceful words of the heart. The collection has been divided into three distinct sections: Love and the Heart, Strength and Courage, and Dedication. From the joy of finding love to the agony of discovering that your beloved does not love you, the emotions that this collection explores will not soon be forgotten.
