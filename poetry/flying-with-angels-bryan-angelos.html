Flying with Angels moves between narrative and lyric in its exploration of love, inspiration, politics and finishes off with a soft landing on spirituality. Bryan Angelos is an aviator by profession and an avid reader of poetry. Voted Poet Laureate of the PaWaR conference 2015. Flying with Angels is Bryan's debut full-length poetry collection and has received fantastic reviews to date. Part of his work, Life is a roller coaster, has been published in the PaWaR magazine and is also due to be published in the Effuse Magazine issue number 4. With this collection he has created a fluid set-piece which is consistent and concise while captivating the reader in entirety. Bryan lives in the UK and you can follow him on Twitter @BryanAngelos.
