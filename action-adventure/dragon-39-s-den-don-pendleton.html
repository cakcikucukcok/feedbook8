The trail leads to Jakarta and the Golden Dragon, a drug lord with his hands in the pockets of officials–and an agenda that goes beyond white powder and cold hard cash. The Executioner hammers the opposition with a vengeance, savaging the Dragon's stranglehold on the drug trade… and engraging a powerful enemy whose mission stops nothing short of full-blown terror.
