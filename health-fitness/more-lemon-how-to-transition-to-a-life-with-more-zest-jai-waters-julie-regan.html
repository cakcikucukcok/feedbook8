MORE LEMON. How to transition to a life with more ZEST is a practical and effective guide that provides applied solutions for people undergoing major life change to successfully transition to a new, happier life.  Whether you are leaving the corporate world to start your own consultancy, want to change career direction, or are currently in transition, this book will take you through the steps that will allow you to learn from the past, let go of it and create a new life based on solid values, a clear life purpose and a compelling and exciting vision for your future.  It is written by two two-experienced change specialists who have both worked through major career transitions and are now in business for themselves.  

The authors share their expertise through the story of Lee, who has recently left her corporate job to go into her own business. The book provides a blueprint for how to create a life filled with more meaning, happiness and joy, and focuses on the skills you need to actively create the life you want. MORE LEMON includes practical exercises and tools and is an ideal resource for anyone going through a major life or work transition.
