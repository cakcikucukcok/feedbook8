As the subject of leadership has lately risen in popularity, a question we can ask is “does character matter?” In this book on the Kings of Judah, there is a very sobering bottom line revealed through the lives of these kings.This book is a brief study of the lives of the twenty kings of Judah with a deeper account of the three greater kings: Jehoshaphat, Hezekiah, and Josiah. Through this study of their lives, decisions, and influence, the reader will discover keys and warnings enabling him to avoid the pitfalls of life and to find and remain on the pathway to eternal life.
