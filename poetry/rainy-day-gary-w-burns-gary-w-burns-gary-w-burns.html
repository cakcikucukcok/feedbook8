The insightful, peaceful and calming poems in this book of poetry are inspiring, speak of wholeness and fosters well-being.  This peaceful poetry celebrates the themes of love, kindness, compassion and harmony.  

In the course of reading through the four sections of Rainy Day: Wondering (Gray Day Wondering, Storms and Things, Raindrops and Windows and Gifts of the Rain) you will experience a relaxing rhythm flowing throughout the expressive and soothing poetry. 

To help slow down a fast pace life style these short one page poems offer a moment of thoughtful reflection and meditative quietness.  This volume of poems makes a wonderful gift for an acquaintance, a friend or a loved one. The volume  is a nice work to have by your favorite chair or your favorite place of reading.

The short calming poems of Rainy Day: Wondering offer a meditative window into mindfulness and personal growth.
