In her latest book, Mastering the Trading Floors, author Judy Coventry presents a fascinating analogy for the Kingdom of God’s advancement by using an economic illustration of the trading floors of the kingdoms.

She transports us back to the beginning of time to unlock mysteries tied to the Tree of Life and the Tree of the Knowledge of Good and Evil and the significance of their seeds. The author proves through captivating revelation that the supernatural seeds from these two trees are still being traded on spiritual trading floors today; determining whether you lay hold of God’s best for your life and advance the Kingdom of God or whether you forfeit your rightful inheritance and end up at a loss!

Biblical character studies throughout the book bring the lessons home by contrasting individuals who prevailed on the trading floors of Heaven and were blessed with those who participated in unrighteous trades and brought disaster upon themselves and their families. Judy journals her own life lessons regarding the trading floors by sharing the riches she has received through wise trades as well as the difficult losses she has experienced from the unfortunate ones.

The revelation contained within Mastering the Trading Floors is biblically researched, timely and extremely strategic. It’s Judy’s desire to see God’s people lay hold of their destinies and receive the wealth of the His Kingdom.  As you read this life-changing book, may you apply the spiritual keys you discover inside to unlock greater blessings and a bountiful harvest for you, your family and your ministry!
