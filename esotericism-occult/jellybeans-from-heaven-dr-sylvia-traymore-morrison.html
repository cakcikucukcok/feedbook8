Have you ever sensed that your loved one in Heaven was looking down on you? 

Have you ever prayed and received an almost immediate answer? 

In Jellybeans from Heaven, Dr. Sylvia Traymore Morrison uses her master story-telling ability to bring us into the wonders of Heaven and God’s magnificent purpose for everything, including jellybeans.  

This inspirational book is a faith-enriching tale of love, tragedy, hope, and redemption. Prepare to catch a glimpse of yourself in characters that ask many of the same questions you have asked about God, life, and life after death.  

As you explore the marvels of Heaven along with new arrival, Carlie, and discover how God uses a bean of sugar to answer prayers and manifest miracles, you will be reminded that He not only hears our prayers but has unique ways of responding to them.   

Never will you look at jelly beans the same again.
