Is exploring Space a choice we make, or something we are driven to do? Is it human nature to stretch our minds, hearts, and for some of us ? bodies ? over the trees, mountains, rainbows, and clouds into the distant world of constellations? Almost every child has spent time lying on his or her back in the hours of darkness, contemplating the appeal of the jewels glittering above in our nighttime sky. In those precious moments, every sparkle feels like a miracle. Lion and Kitsune, integrates those experiences into a creative and educational literary tool that will elicit wonder from its readers, while it teaches the specifics of space and star systems. Rocket through this book with Lion and Kitsune, a space exploration team, on a megastar tour of shapes, colors, and other celestial phenomena. Become a Space Imagineer. Fly straight into the shining spectacle of the sky. It's easy and fun... and you don't even need a spacecraft!
