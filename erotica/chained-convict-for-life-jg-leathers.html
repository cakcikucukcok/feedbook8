Although it's just a 'fantasy' to start, it's not long before Thomas creates a set of full-body restraints - a Stainless Steel Restraint And Discipline Harness. Once locked in this personalized prison, Sabrina is utterly helpless to remove it and can only struggle uselessly - regretting her foolish desire to be so securely confined and controlled.
