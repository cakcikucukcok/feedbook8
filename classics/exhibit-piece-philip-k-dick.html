Exhibit Piece - Philip K. Dick | "Exhibit Piece" is a 1954 science fiction short story by American writer Philip K. Dick. The story is an early exploration of the concept of shifting realities, a common theme in Dick's subsequent works. The protagonist is a future historian of the 20th century and finds himself shifting in time from the future to that time period.
