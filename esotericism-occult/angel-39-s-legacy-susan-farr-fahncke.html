What would you do if this was your last year on earth? What if someone you couldnt live without had only one year? How would you live that year?

We have all been impacted by cancer. Some of us have battled it. Others have fought alongside loved ones in their own battle. Angel was a warrior, whose fight with cancer changed her for the better. With deep compassion and gratitude, Angel lived each day with great caring for others. Her joyous love for others and for life left a great many lessons for us all.

Angels Legacy is a finding of what really matters and also what doesnt. It is having peace amid pain. It is having faith despite fear and loving others above yourself. Angels Legacy shows you how to live in happiness despite your own personal pain and challenges.

Whether you are a caregiver, are fighting cancer, or simply love someone who is, Angels Legacy is for you.

Her story will lift you and find you asking yourself, what will your legacy be?
