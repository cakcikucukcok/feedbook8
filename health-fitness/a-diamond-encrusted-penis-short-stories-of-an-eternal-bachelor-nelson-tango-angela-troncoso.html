Have you ever imagined faking your perspective on marriage and the possibility of having children?  Alex is a young and powerful man on a quest to be sexually involved with women from all over the world.  His views on life have been shaped by continuous sentimental failures.  This eternal bachelor takes you on a journey through his collection of short stories and lustful trophies.  Freedom, infidelity, debauchery, and toxic relationships will be dissected throughout this book.

Do you think that you really know your significant other?  Nelson Tango presents a thesis on why getting married and having children are not the only avenues towards happiness but the by-product of the pressures of society.  He also shares personal experiences of failed relationships that led him to believe that humans can't remain monogamous for extended periods of time.

Author's Biography

Nelson Tango was born in Cartagena, Colombia.  Throughout his life, he has moved to a different city every year as he enjoys constant change.  At the age of 18, he came to the United States by himself in search of new opportunities with only $100 dollars in his pocket.  Tango went to college to study Business Administration but dropped out after the second year in order to become an entrepreneur.  In 2008, Tango joined the U.S. Marines and served honorably during 8 years.  He has multiple combat tours to Afghanistan as an Infantryman.  During his military career, Tango obtained a Bachelor of Science in Criminal Justice Administration and a Masters degree in Forensic Science.  Tango has lived in Alaska, Florida, South Carolina, California, North Carolina and Virginia.

Early in 2016, Tango sold all of his belongings and decided to go on a world book writing tour.  This book was written from more than 40 different countries.  Nelson Tango currently lives as a nomad globetrotter.  He is single, free spirited and the creator of the Tango Foundation, a charity for poor kids around the world.
