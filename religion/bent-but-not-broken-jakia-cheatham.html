When Jakia found out she had to have spinal surgery, she didnt have any idea how momentous her surgery would be. Not only did it change her life, but it changed the lives of the people around her as well. She realized nothing in life is permanent. In this book Jakia reveals her secrets to coping with scoliosis. She talks about how her condition turned her life upside down and tested her faith. Through scriptures and prayer Jakia emphasizes that scoliosis can only bend you, but never break you.

Do you have scoliosis, or do you know someone with it? Do you have a condition and you need help coping? Are you a teen and society has you questioning life? This book can lift your spirit, change your heart, and encourage you to exercise your faith. This book aims to inspire the whole family.
