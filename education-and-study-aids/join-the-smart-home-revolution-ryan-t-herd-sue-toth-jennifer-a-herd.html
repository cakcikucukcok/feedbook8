Join the Smart Home Revolution is a captivating book about enjoying a simplified technology lifestyle in the comfort of your own home.  From an investment point of view, homes with technology are perceived to be better kept, sell faster, and command significantly more money than homes without technology.  Ryan is a noble servant to the home technology industry... lending his time and expertise to several volunteer roles to help foster an understanding of the CEDIA channel, and this book is one more way he’s helping continue the conversation. Ryan is a subject matter expert  for CEDIA, the National Association of Home Builders (NAHB), the American Society of Interior Designers (ASID), the American Institute of Architects (AIA), and Coldwell Banker Real Estate, just to name a few.  Ryan serves as the New Jersey State Captain for Government Affairs at CEDIA and I have had the privilege of working closely with him for years, sharing our philosophies of delivering exceptional experiences to homeowners. This book will help homeowners understand that a technology lifestyle is easier to have than they think.
