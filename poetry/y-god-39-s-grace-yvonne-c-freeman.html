Y Gods Grace is a motivational book of inspirational poetry that depicts the human struggle of right and wrong, in this world full of chaos and turmoil. These poetic expressions of Gods love and grace are told in the testimonies of everyday people like you and me. Y Gods Grace stresses how Gods word, as it is written in the Holy Bible, can bring peace and clarity to your life. As you take this journey with me through the untold stories of people like yourself, youll experience their trials and tribulations, triumphs and failures; the struggle between a materialistic or spiritual life, testing their faith. Theres a fine line between good and evil; a slippery slope, easily crossed in this world of greed and lust. Y Gods Grace is a book of thought provoking stories and poems that will engage you, entertain you and challenge you, to be a better you. Y Gods Grace is the second book written by Yvonne C. Freeman, inspired by Gods amazing grace, as it is so appropriately named. Yvonne named the books, Y her signature, as opposed to Why; due to the on-going curiosity of her inner child who still questions this world around her. Y Spiritual Journey was Yvonnes first book published May 2008. Go to www.wordsofspirit.com to read some poetry excerpts.
