This innovative guide will explain how to have self-confidence and to relax and enjoy the date. Some dating books help a certain kind of guy looking for a certain kind of girl. Others will explain how to get the girl, but not how to actually build up the confidence to do it. The primary purpose of Dating Basics 101 is to assist in gaining the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in the world of dating.
