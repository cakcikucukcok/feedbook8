The Novel Obstacles is the story about a young man, who at the onset , was sure of himself and strongly believed that he has all it takes to succeed in life having broken through the concrete walls of poverty and deprivation at the early stages of his life. Strong character, drive and luck; all helped him to reach the pinnacle of success and fulfilment at an early stage in his life. But ironically and precipitously too, obstacles in form of distractions - lacking focus - crept in and turned his life upside down; sending him on a downward spin, nearly to the abyss. It is the story of small obstacles leading to big falls.
