  • Tricks and techniques for creating depth and dimension using lines (linear perspective), values (atmospheric perspective) and color.
  • 23 mini demonstrations on how to apply basic principles of perspective make getting started easy.
  • 12 full step-by-step demonstrations, each with a structural drawing, cover a variety of subjects, including interiors, architecture and still lifes.
  • Starting with a simple box to illustrate fundamental concepts, artists will develop a working understanding of how to cast any subject in realistic depth and dimension. This book puts it all into perspective.
