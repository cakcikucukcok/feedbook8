Tasked with uncovering the fate of several women who’d mysteriously disappeared on a horse ranch, Linda sets up camp and takes turns watching…and waiting.

Just as she’s falling asleep, something whispers in her mind. Maybe she should wake the other watcher and alert him. But by then it was too late.

She was his.

Excerpt:

"This is where you tracked them?" Linda, a 19-year-old detective, viewed the scenic landscape of the vast ranch.

"Yep." The burly cowboy drawled, pushing back his hat.

"How?" Linda muttered more to herself than him, scanning the area.

"Don't know." He kicked the ground. "All I know is that I hired three young women and they vanished."

"They just abandoned their belongings?" The wheels in her mind were spinning. But she couldn't find any answers.

He nodded.

Linda knew they had to have been kidnapped. But at night? Why? Who would have done this? What was the motive?

"They slipped from their rooms, late at night and weren't seen again." He said.

"All three vanished here? Within weeks of each other?" Linda tapped her cheeks inspecting the tracks, which stopped at the pile of clothes.

"Yep."

"I'll stay here tonight and see what happens." Linda stated, knowing whatever happened to the ladies might happen to her, but she'd be ready.

"I'll stay with you and see what happens. We'll set up camp, because I want answers too." His voice shook through her thoughts and she nodded.

Soon, the full moon cast its silver light upon the land. SIlence filled the starry night. Linda sat on a rock before a crackling fire, watching and waiting on her shift, the cowboy snored in a sleeping bag a mere five feet away. She'd wake him if anything happened.

It was so peaceful out here and it was hard to think that there was anything wrong. She yawned, bored and sleepy and closed her eyes momentarily.

Come to me. A voice suddenly rang in her head, compelling her to obey.

Shaking her head, she rose to her feet. She was definitely hearing things. Maybe she should wake him up and snatch some sleep.

Linda, however, only listened to the deep, masculine voice in her head, and forgot everything else.

Take off your clothes.

She obeyed. They fluttered to the ground as a large, powerful feral stallion silently emerged from the trees. He was dark brown and his eyes were so masterful she desired to do whatever he willed.

He pranced to her and reared on his hind legs as she trembled at his power. She panted as her heat rose within her like a slow burning fire.
