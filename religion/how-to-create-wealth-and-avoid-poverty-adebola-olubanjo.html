The purpose of this book is to:

motivate people to take steps to create wealth for themselves and their loved ones;

rally people to avoid poverty;

provide a tool for teaching people to create and accumulate wealth;

show people how to maintain the wealth that they have created or has been handed over to them;

show parents how to ensure that their children create wealth and maintain and multiply the wealth in their possession;

motivate people to do something that will allow them to live above the poverty level, no matter their age, environment, or country;

make people cooperate with the Creatorthe almighty Godin the work of creation and multiplication of wealth; and

demystify as much as possible the process of wealth creation and accumulation.
