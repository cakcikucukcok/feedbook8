This is a very funny and romantic original novel with fascinating characters, adventure and mystery from the year 2005-2008.

Out of boredom, Nadine, a single 48 year old secretary who lives in Chelsea, Massachusetts, is seeking her goal of fame and fortune. Her jealous boss, Larry, an accountant who is married, rejects her idea in fear of losing her.

After Larry and Nadine fall madly in love, they have spontaneous sex on the desk in his office and get caught and fired. Larry leaves his wife and moves to Vegas with Nadine where her wacky 70 year old Uncle Willie lives.

While in Vegas, Nadine meets a big gambler, Snake, and Larry gets very jealous. Nadine and Snake are living the high life of big fortune and luxury which is part of her goal. Snake suddenly and mysteriously dies, so she wonders what to do next and soon comes up with an idea.

Meanwhile, Larry and Nadine make new plans. After awhile, all hell breaks loose from their big parties, another very mysterious death and there is a robbery of Nadines most valuable jewels. Later on, another robbery takes place. The police continue throughout this story to try and find the thief who robbed her jewels and to solve the mysterious death.

Nadine gets involved in show business even though Larry rejects her goal in fear of losing her. He later pursues a new career. After she encounters trouble in show business, they take a Beverly Hills, CA vacation and get caught up in movie making. They soon return to Vegas. When the story nears its end, Nadine gets very surprising news about her stolen jewels. She also attends a hilarious double wedding ceremony, and she later has another big jewel surprise. In addition to all of the funny characters, Nadines spontaneous love is a riot and so is Larrys love making.

LARRY AND NADINE IN VEGAS is a comic romp bursting with one-liners. Its wild, wacky and wonderful and filled with laughs from start to finish. This is a funny story that draws you in. ITS A FASCINATING COMEDY!
