AQUARIUS

Discover what lies ahead of you in 2014. Join world renowned astrologer Dadhichi as he takes you on a journey into your future.

As an Aquarian, you think and behave differently from everyone else, living wholly and solely on your own terms. Your unconventional nature, along with your curious insights, lead to the creation of original ideas. However, despite your quest for individuality, you can be afraid to express your dreams and ambitions for fear of ridicule. With the use of this book you will learn which star signs can satisfy your need for freedom and change, how to make the most of your natural ingenuity and dynamism in the workplace and what to expect in 2014.

Your stars will reveal

• Monthly and daily predictions for 2014.

• Insights into the Aquarian woman, man and child.

• Which star signs you are best matched with as well as who to avoid in the areas of love, friendship and the workplace.

• Ways to harness your career potential and tips for financial success.

• How to optimise your health and beauty.
