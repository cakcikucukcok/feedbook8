A wormhole away from Earth, a planet square in the goldilocks zone was found - a perfect location for a new colony. Yet for the first colonists, this alien world is plagued by a saboteur.

When Gary Holbrook signed on as the colony’s doctor, there was a catch - the mission was for married couples only. Without a suitable spouse, he reluctantly married Margo Murphy, a grubby entomologist with a fondness for butterflies. This morning Gary watched his wife die

The crash of their colony ship was their introduction to their new world. Accident after accident claimed the lives of more and more colonists. Margo’s death seemed like just more of the same bad luck until Gary discovered she had found evidence of a saboteur in their midst. Her investigation exposed secret motives hidden from the colonists that would change everything.

Now Gary must follow Margo’s clues to stop the saboteur before it’s too late.
