This book challenges you to see beyond your immediate circumstances and to act in step with your life goals. As a result of studying this book, the reader will be familiar with principles of the Better Choice Process. Upon completion of this book, the reader should better appreciate the strengths of the analytical approach to decision making and be able to apply the methods presented in real life. The Better Choice Process is easy to learn for anyone who is ready, has a little experience, and has a real desire to be effective in making better choices. The key ingredient in the success of this process is your commitment to doing the right thing, the right way, for the right reason, expecting the right results to benefit others according to the principles of high integrity.
