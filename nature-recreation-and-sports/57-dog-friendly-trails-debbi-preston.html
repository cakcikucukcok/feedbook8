Trails change! Since writing my two guidebooks, 48 Dog-Friendly Trails and Dog-Friendly Trails for All Seasons, I found some wonderful new destinations and new trails to get to some old favorites. Thus, 57 Dog-Friendly Trails: In Californias Foothills and the Sierra Nevada evolved covering fifty-seven trailheads, some with multiple hiking options. All of the trails remain dog friendly, and all have great destinations. Californias foothills and the Sierra Nevada are lush with a variety of trails taking you to different venueswildflowers, waterfalls, alpine lakes, meadows, snow, and flowing rivers.
