I Am an Indigo Child Hear My Words is a dialogue of messages from the Indigo herself, delivered with the unity of other Indigos and the universal energy of spirit. She challenges the need for change with the global state of humanity government greed, war and religion through persistence in her words. A powerful network of likeminded Indigos will hold the power to transition through change and together reunite as it was in the beginning. The natural gifts Indigos possess including telepathy, energy channelling, clairvoyance and mediumship, remain the dual tools to resonate the world one individual at a time. The need to call out to these people promotes hope in world peace once again, and to do this the answers are simply in remembering who we really are.

A powerful message dedicated to those gifted beings, unveiling the truth about our existence, our purpose on Earth, and making the connection with humanity.
