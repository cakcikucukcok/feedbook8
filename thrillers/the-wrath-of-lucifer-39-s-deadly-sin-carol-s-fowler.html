Picture a woman riding a motorcycle alone on a deserted country road along the cornfields of northern Illinois. She runs out of gas. It's getting dark and there's only one house close enough where she may be able to get help. When she knocks at the door for assistance, her worst nightmare comes to life. Cheyanne is captured and imprisoned by a maniacal serial killer. The woman is beaten, raped, and subjected to cruel mental and physical abuse. But Cheyanne is a 20-year veteran of the Marines and knows how to fight back. Her captor is an abusive drunk who takes pleasure in hurting women. There's no guarantee Cheyanne will get out of this situation alive, but she must try. Will her survival instincts save her? Whatever did Cheyanne do to deserve The Wrath of Lucifer's Deadly Sin? This extraordinary nail-biter will leave you breathless until the very last page.
