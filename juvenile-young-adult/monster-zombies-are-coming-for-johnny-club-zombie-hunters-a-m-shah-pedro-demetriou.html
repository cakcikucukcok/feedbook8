At Sandy Furlow Middle School, something strange is happening.  Johnny and Alex have realized that every day that passes, another little boy or girl goes missing.  It turns out that the evil witch Ms. Eisenstein is back! This time she came with an army to destroy Johnny and his sidekick Alex for good. 

What Johnny and Alex don’t realize is that Ms. Eisenstein has a secret weapon that will turn all the little boys and girls from Sandy Furlow Middle School into brain-dead zombie robots. 

Will Johnny and Alex have the power to fight off the powerful witch, Ms. Eisenstein, and her monster zombies?  It's the ultimate battle of spell casters and monster zombies versus Johnny and Alex in Club Zombie Hunters!
