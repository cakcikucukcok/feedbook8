She and her younger sister, Melanata, live with their grandmother in a charming town. Perfectly typical, right? And, sure, not many teenagers have visions...er...dreams of the future and of the past, like Shea does, but that can be ignored. Everything is normal. Until... Aaron and Diana Aster.
