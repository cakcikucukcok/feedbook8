The body of Ben Haversham's Uncle Izzy vanishes and Ben, although he attended the funeral, suspects his uncle is still alive.  Ben's suspicion is confirmed when he's told that his uncle was working undercover to bring to justice a group of iconoclasts who totalled the town's Big Prawn.    

Uncle Izzy's cover was blown becaused he broke the rules by visiting Ben on his birthday, and the fear is that the iconoclasts might exact their revenge by harming Ben!

Ben discovers the whereabouts of two missing archaeology students who vanished from a local derelict mansion, Farewell Manor, and he also solves the mystery of what happened to the manor's owner, Alfie Henderson, who vanished without a trace in 1983.  This mystery solving attracts publicity and places Ben in danger, making him an easy target for the iconoclasts, who kidnap him in order to flush out his uncle.    

Will Uncle Izzy emerge from hiding and rescue his nephew?
