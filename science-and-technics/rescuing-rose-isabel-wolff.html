Rose Costelloe LOVES problems - other people's problems that is.  As an agony aunt she adores dishing out advice on everything from dizziness to divorce.  But this rather thorny Rose is not so good when it comes to her own life  Her new marriage is in shreds, she's had to move house, and the bills are piling up.  Then her best friends, twins Bella and Bea, suggest that Rose lets out her spare room and Theo, a geeky accountant with a secret passion seems the perfect roommate.  But just as things are looking up, Rose's universe is thrown into chaos. Her husband reappears, her editor demands 'more sex!' and worse, she's being stalked.  In all this one person alone grasps what really matters to Rose beneath her prickly exterior.  Will she at last be able to address the huge problem that she faces in her own life?  This upbeat and poignant tale is the newly reissued fourth novel from the internationally best-selling author of 'A Vintage Affair'.
