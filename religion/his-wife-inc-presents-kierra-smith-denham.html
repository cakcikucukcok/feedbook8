His Wife, Inc., is a nonprofit organization that God instructed me to establish. The mission is to provide a safe haven where women can come together for personal development, which positively impacts their roles as wives. You can effectively execute your role as His Wife, once you understand who you were created to be as a woman. His Wife, Inc., assists women in all areas of human service, as well as spiritual guidance. His Wife, Inc., also incorporates men several times a year to build and strengthen the marriage/relationship as a whole.

The Marriage Consultant is not solely for wives, it is also for husbands, and couples headed in the direction of marriage. This book describes the highs and lows of marriage from both gender perspectives. It gives guidance and resolution for situations that are encountered in marriage that many people refuse to discuss. The Marriage Consultant exhibits how to build your house, pray for your spouse, and fulfill the purpose of your marriage.
