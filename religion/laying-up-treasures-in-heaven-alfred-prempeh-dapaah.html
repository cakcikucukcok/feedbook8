As a child of God and a citizen of the Kingdom of Heaven, do you know how you are connected to heaven on as far as your daily life is concerned? But lay up for yourselves treasures in heaven Where neither moth nor rust doth corrupt And where thieves do not break through nor steal For where your treasure is, there will your heart be also Matthew 6:20-21
