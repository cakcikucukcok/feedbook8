Former US Special Forces operative, Jake Walker, is in Bolivia finishing some consulting work when he gets a phone call and must fly home immediately.  Lucky for him, the youngest daughter of  the former US President is flying back to Houston on her father’s private Gulf Stream jet and she offers him a lift.

A gang of Colombians with half a ton of cocaine await a rendezvous with a buyer in one of the most dangerous places on the planet, the Darien Province, which spans the border between Colombia and Panama.

Steaming through the Panama Canal is the USS Peleliu, on her way home after six months at sea.

Bringing everyone together is a high level al-Qaeda operative intent on revenge.

Featuring battle scenes in Afghanistan and Iraq and present day action in Central America, this fast paced thriller takes us on a journey we will never forget.
