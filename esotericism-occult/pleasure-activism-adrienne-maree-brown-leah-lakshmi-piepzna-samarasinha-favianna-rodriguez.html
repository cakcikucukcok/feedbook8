We've decided to launch an "Emergent Strategy" book series to build on the success of adrienne's book of the same name. Some books in the series will be written or edited by adrienne herself and some will be written/edited by others. This is the first book in that series.

The contributors are groundbreaking social change agents in a variety of fields, writing from a range of identities. In different ways, they all draw upon and present the black feminist tradition, making it relevant to a modern audience.

adrienne has built a devoted following with previous titles, Emergent Strategy (14,000 sold) and Octavia's Brood (25,000 sold). She stays in touch with that audience through her blog, social media, and her "Pleasure Dome" column in Bitch magazine, and has been stoking their interest in this title.
