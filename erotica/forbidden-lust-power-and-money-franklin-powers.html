As Kyle Stone vacillates between the past and the present and struggles to find is way out of the darkness with help from a witch and a white candle, now only time will tell if he will fall deeper into the darkness or emerge to help others who have fallen and lost their way. Kyle now loaded with ten black candles and one white candle is ready to do battle with all who want to do him harm.
