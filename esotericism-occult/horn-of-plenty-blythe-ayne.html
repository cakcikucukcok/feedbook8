HORN OF PLENTY – The Cornucopia of Your Life by Dr. Blythe Ayne is overflowing with information about living a prosperous life, within peace and joy.

What do you want to have pour out of your horn of plenty? It’s all waiting to come into your life. But first the blocks must be removed. HORN OF PLENTY – The Cornucopia of Your Life shows you how these blocks are built, and empowers you with ways in which to remove them. This first requires an understanding of what is at cause and what is at effect in your life. 

People commonly think of the effects in their lives as causes. This is crippling. HORN OF PLENTY shows the reader how to correct this confusion. You will soon stand before your own horn of plenty, ready to receive all that is waiting to come into your life. 

This inspirational reading will bring about a wonderful shift in you toward happiness, fulfillment, prosperity and joy. The “Points for Contemplation and Action” that follow each chapter will guide you in putting this important information into practice.
