1. From the development of the agricultural revolutions, the first indication with the hidden values of the division of entirety was formed with the division of social status with different works, and the rapid growth of the population was headed along with the conversion of the production manners, which will lead toward the innovations of the systematical formation of the economy in later developments. With the growth of the populations in the clans, the clans could determine the trend of the city with the further indication of the metropolis.

Under the systematical innovation of the production manner in agricultural revolutions, the surplus could form within the union of the clans; thus, the idlers or the populations who didn't work for the basic-necessities could be separated out from the secular work into other division of social status; under the massive range of the corporation between different individuals in the cognitive revolution, which led toward the bonding of imaginary world with the reality, the unavoidable conflicts between the ideologies, which were considered to be the totem worship from the matter of natural environment with the pursuit of understanding of reality, will produce the torch leader for the clans; thus, the government formed; under the other division of social status for idlers in order to maintain the bonding for the different individuals in the massive corporation, the religious values could be formed in order to extend the strength of the corporations.

With the different environmental factors of reality for the subjects to survive, the different systematical formation for the innovation along with the clans could form such as distribution of powers of Anglo Saxons and centralization of powers of German, which the analysis of the examples was indicated in the work of “Foundation of Uniformity with Modernization” (refer to [4] in the reference).

Yet, no matter how will the history determine the path of the development for the civilizations, the principles of the modern economy won't change: the division of labor creates efficiency, which was true under whatever kind of government and society: decentralization or totalitarianism. 

Through the reflection of the history of the development of civilization of humankind, the advanced development from the perspective of the evolution could be determined through the intelligence, which was expressed as the cultural orders in human civilization and natural orders in the wild animals. The Law of the Jungle, as the survival of the fittest, will no longer apply to the human civilization because of the artificial choices from the values of humankind with the satisfaction. For example, the ancestors will keep the digestible crops for the clans and further sowing for the growth of populations and digestible meats from the animal husbandry in order for obtaining the nutrients; yet, the wheat domesticated humankind and became the most successful species in the global scale including pig, cows, chickens and so on. Moreover, the deviation of the natural selections, which was the principle of the survival of the fittest, was driven with the economic prosperity with the widener and widener corporation between the different individual and different clans.

With the result of the communication with the massive corporation between different individual, the economic prosperity, which was also decided the further remnants of the civilization of a special groups or clans. Videlicet, the economic prosperity along with the social stability overrode all the principles and cultures in order to seek for the pure indication of common values, which were the economic interests and survival, and the creation of the advanced cultures with the absolute standard of the economic prosperity, scientific discoveries, and technological innovations could be produced through it.
