This new book is actually a revision and expansion of the author’s previously published cookbook.  It was so well received that people wanted more – more books, and more recipes.  So, here it is.

What people really loved about this book is that it is not your usual cookbook.  This one has a dual purpose.  It not only offers you great recipes to prepare.  It tells you who to prepare them for.

This book contains complete dinner menus for each astrological sign, as well as a birthday cake for each sign.  There are also recipes from people of each of the signs plus some of the authors favorite recipes, all reflecting their unique personalities and characteristics.

 

Since each sun sign rules various herbs and spices, flavors and foods, preceding each dinner menu you will find a brief description of each sign’s traits and suggested foods and flavors.

In addition to giving you some yummy recipes to put in your tummy, this book will put some smiles on your face.  It is a fun trip around the zodiac and the kitchen.

This is the author’s fifth book, all of which met with very positive reactions, but this is the probably the most popular one.

You will not only want a copy for yourself, but you will definitely want several for gifts.
