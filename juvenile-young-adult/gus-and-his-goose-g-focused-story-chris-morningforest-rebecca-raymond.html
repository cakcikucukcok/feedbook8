Gus' goose lives in his garage. Gus and his goose love to go out in all kinds of weather. In this delightful, rhyming, "G" focused story, you will meet Gus' brother who raises geese, Glenda who raises grapes, and Harley Happy who loves to help in the garden. Young readers get lots of practice on the sound the letter "G" makes. Activities at the end and looking for Baby Goose on every page keep young readers entertained. This is one in our series of letter focused books. Another popular one is Little Lacy Ladybug (guess which letter!). Look for them on this and other internet sites. Search Chris Morningforest.
