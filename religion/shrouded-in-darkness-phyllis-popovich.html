Shrouded in Darkness by Phyllis Popovich is an intriguing read. She writes on the conspiracy theory, which is my passion; and, she writes on the end-time apostasy of the Church, which is the last great sign of Christs coming. Phyllis deals with the end time plight of Israel, which every Christian should know. Our Lord has revealed to her future events that will occur, which I cannot speak to, because I have not experienced visions. John Daniel, author of Scarlet and the Beast.

The result of over 40 years research, Shrouded in Darkness reveals a conspiracy, at which most scoff, but which has spanned nearly 6000 years from the time of Lucifers fall until the present day. It exposes the plot designed by Lucifer (otherwise referred to as Satan) in his attempt to enthrone Antichrist bringing the world under his tyrannical rule. His influence within the political and spiritual arenas, and his agenda intended to deceive and retain the title deed of the world which was lost in Eden, is minutely depicted.

Fully persuaded that God has had foreknowledge of every diabolical device of Satan and his minions, and in Gods wisdom, He is allowing them to reach the end of their own resources, Shrouded in Darkness, is one individuals attempt to reveal the enemys underhanded tactics in an effort to point men and women back to Jesus Christ and the truth of the Gospel.
