As you may have noticed, marriage is in crisis! Experts tell us that 50% or more of marriages in the U.S. end in divorce. Living together prior to getting married increases the likelihood of getting divorced by 40 percent. Forty-three percent of American children are being raised without their fathers.

Out of this sad situation comes a question: Can marriage really be the time of your life?

Tom Klobucher’s answer is, “Absolutely!” But we need to think about marriage differently from the way most people do. Tom’s newest book comes to us after over half a century in an engaged, fulfilled and thriving marriage. Tom has something incredible to say, and it may not be like anything you have heard before.

What really matters, Tom says, isn’t what you expect to get out of the relationship, but what you expect to bring into it. The best marriage doesn’t start with you looking outward at your spouse. Instead, Tom invites you to look inward, at yourself.

You see, the best marriage is a transformational marriage, and it really is the time of your life!

What is a transformational marriage, and how can you have one? Please read on to discover Tom’s 24 steps to a happy, positive, and engaged marriage—and start having the time of your life!

The best marriage is not a transaction—it’s a transformation!
