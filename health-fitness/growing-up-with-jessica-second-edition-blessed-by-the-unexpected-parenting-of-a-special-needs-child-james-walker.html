A moving true story, shared, by Jessica's Dad. He honestly shares the ups and downs of his wife and family, as they quite unexpectedly, began grappling with every parent's worst nightmare.

Since that frosty Halloween night, when Jim's wife Renée quietly whispered with tears in her eyes, "Something is wrong with Jessica," they have been shaken to the very roots of their faith, found healing and victory, as they all 'grew up with Jessica.'

"...we beheld the privilege we had been given... embracing the joys and blessings of providing ultimate caregving for another human soul."  James Walker

You will experience, their breath taking roller coaster ride, of tragedy, love, hope and faith and emerge touched and inspired. This is a heartwarming book where ultimately faith overcomes tragedy.
