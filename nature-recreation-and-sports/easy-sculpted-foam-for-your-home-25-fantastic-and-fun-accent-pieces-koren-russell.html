Easy Sculpted Foam for Your Home

The process is so fun it won't be long until your home is filled with decorative accents with the look of carved wood, sculpted clay, or poured ceramics.

  • Fabulous new techniques for working with foam
  • 25 fun home decor projects to make
  • Clear, easy step-by-step directions; patterns included
