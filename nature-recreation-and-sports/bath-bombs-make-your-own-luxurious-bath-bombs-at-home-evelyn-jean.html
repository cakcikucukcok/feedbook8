How often have we yearned for a warm bath at the end of a stressful day? Bath bombs are a hard packed mixture of moisturizers, aromas, colors and many other ingredients that make your bath time more relaxing. You can easily add in essential oils, fragrance, colors and herbs. If you were to purchase bath bombs at a health and beauty store, you could easily spend $4 to $6 on each one. Depending on the brand, the price can easily skyrocket up to nearly $9 per bath bomb. Even though the recipes in this book make large batches of bath bombs, when you do the math, each bath bomb cost a fraction of the price and is made especially for you, by you, and you know exactly what is it. By making your own bath bombs at home, you can control the type and quality of ingredients that go into every product. This book contains 80 pages of recipes for homemade bath bombs, including a multitude of aromatherapy based bath bombs. Keep your eyes open for other DIY, money saving books from Evelyn!
