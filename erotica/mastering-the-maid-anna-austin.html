~~~~~ PG Excerpt ~~~~~

 

"Good evening my dear." He gave a low bow. "I trust you are well. Perhaps you would like a glass of wine?" He indicated towards the open bottle on the side-table. "I drink only the best."

 

 "Yes sir, I would love some wine. Thank you" she said politely and met his eyes for a second.

 

He filled a glass and handed it to her. It looked oversize in her petite hands. She really was a delicate little thing. He wondered whether she would be able to take what he had in store for her, or whether he would break her.

 

He poured a glass and took a sip. It tasted good, but then his senses were always heightened before he indulged in sexual depravity.

 

"So, Isabel. I will repeat what I said to you earlier. I am your master, and you a mere servant to my whims and desires. Whatever I ask of you, you will obey. Do you understand?"

 

 Isabel's hands trembled as she held the wine glass. She took a small sip of the wine and delighted in its taste. It was fruity and fragrant and she smiled. "Yes. I understand sir."

 

"Good. Because at times tonight, you may be frightened. Terrified even. But cruel though I undoubtedly am, I am not a monster. Your life belongs to me, but I will not end it. Indeed, perhaps you will find something wakened within you that you did not know existed. But if not, I will give you a safe-word to use. If you utter this word, I will let you leave at once. The word is 'Lucifer.' Do you understand?”

 

He gestured to her wine glass. "Finish it. You may find some courage in it."
