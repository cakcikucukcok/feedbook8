FBI analyst Imogen Trager, the determined heroine of Faithless Elector, has not given up her fight for the truth. She must fight against time, a sinister network, and even her own colleagues to stop the conspiracy to seize the presidency.

If it succeeds, the incoming President will install a pliant Attorney General, in order to have the case dropped -- and those behind the conspiracy will have won. The integrity of politics in the United States lies in the hands of Imogen and the group of colleagues she thinks she can trust. But can she?

She joins forces with Trey Kelly, an FBI network specialist, like her, an outsider at the bureau. Together, they struggle to find the back door the conspirators may have left unlocked. They’ll have to hurry. There’s only a month until the inauguration of a new president.
