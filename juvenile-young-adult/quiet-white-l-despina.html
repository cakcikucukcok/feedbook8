Quiet White L: The Promise A Colourful Companion (A Picture Book and Teaching Resource) This is the colour picture book of Quiet White L, The Promise. It is suitable for all ages, with more pictures than reading text. Do you prefer more pictures than text? This is a book for you! This picture book contains twenty-five colour illustration and two black-and-white drawings, which you can colour in yourself. Are you a teacher or other adult who wants to encourage your students to think about storytelling and writing, about structure, plot, characters, and motives for actions through the use of pictures? This is a book for you! At the back of this book, you will find over four full pages of questions and suggestions to enable you to stimulate your students imaginations, to encourage and enable them to tell and write their own stories. It includes comprehension questions to use alongside the full length edition of Quiet White L: The Promise. This is more than just a picture book; it is a teaching aid. This is more than just a teachers aid; it is an adventure for you to enter into and enjoy!
