Someone stealing millions is willing to kill to succeed.

     Kate Killoy can’t wait to pick a puppy from the first litter sired by her champion Samoyed Dillon and a cross country trip with her fiancé Harry Foyle to visit his old professor will be the frosting on the cake.

     However plans go awry when Kate finds herself being chased by a convict. Harry must fight both to keep her safe and keep his mentor, now falsely accused of theft, from going to prison.

     As love blooms among their friends, Harry and Kate's relationship is threatened both by a secret Kate’s hiding and a shooter who wants them both dead for threatening to block the massive theft of millions of dollars.

      Fleeing back across the country, with puppy in tow, Kate and Harry mount a final battle to stop the thief turned assassin whose rage now threatens everyone they hold dear. 

     There is high adventure in this second book in the Kate Killoy Mystery Series. Book one is Fashion Goes to the Dogs, high drama in NYC during the dog show.
