From Broken to Blest: Embracing the Healing that Awaits You, by Adele M. Gill and Dr. Verna Benner Carson is a true life story about author Adele Gill who grew up with a mother who was chronically and acutely mentally ill, and a father, whom she later learned, was involved in the Mafia. As a result of the untreated childhood trauma she endured, Adele struggled with depression and a neuro-autoimmune condition for 35 years until she realized only God could bring her healing—and He did!

This book is about how Adele, with God’s help, was able to move from broken to blest, helping her to grow in hope, and to embrace the healing that awaited her. From Broken to Blest is a must-read for anyone challenged by the far-reaching effects of untreated childhood trauma including depression, PTSD, auto-immune and/or certain neurological conditions, which often go hand in hand. 

From Broken to Blest will help readers to recognize the effects of untreated childhood trauma in their own lives, and enable them to begin the healing process through The Healing Workshop. This book can help ease depression and auto-immune symptoms, often associated with untreated childhood trauma, and assist readers move from broken to blest as they embrace their own healing, through what the authors call the Mind-Body-Faith Phenomenon.
