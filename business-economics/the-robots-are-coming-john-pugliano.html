How you can survive (and even thrive) during the artificial intelligence-powered robot takeover of the workplace

No longer just a concern for factory workers, intelligent robots are replacing workers in previously secure occupations. For college-educated professionals, the risk of being supplanted by smart automation” grows every day. You might think your job is safe, but is it? Vital reading for millions, The Robots are Coming is the first guide aimed at helping you personally understand, predict, and navigate the new smart economy. Offering actionable steps for remaining essential to the future economy, this helpful guide breaks down current technology and automation trends into layman's terms. It specifically details how and why jobs are being put at risk. Most importantly, The Robots are Coming incorporates a detailed action plan for becoming invaluable at work and avoiding being replaced.
