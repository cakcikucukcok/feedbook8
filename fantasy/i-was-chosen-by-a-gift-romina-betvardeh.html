When helping a random man on the street Sandy never thought her life will change for ever. But months later the man found her and gave her an ancient old looking gift.

A story full of challenges and magic, where the powerful bully the weak and the rich press down on those less fortunate without being aware that the cycle of life is vicious and it will turn its wheels against them.
