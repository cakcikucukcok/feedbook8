Poems of Angst and Rebirth is a collection of poems that journey into the mind of a Generation Xer attempting to navigate her way through a disconnected world. Seeking solace, meaning, and hope from the crossroads between visualizations of the physical world and the creative word, these poems touch on themes of despair, isolation, heartbreak, longing to be desired, sociopolitical affairs, self-actualization, and reawakening to the need for a fulfilling life. The poems are to be read by all who seek validation of their emotions during periods of loneliness, strength to transcend adversity and angst, the cathartic effect of reflecting in the midst of our natural environment, and the inspiration to achieve connection in a disconnected world.
