BE P.R.O.U.D explores the process of mentorship and presents practical tools for facilitating the relationship. If you've ever been curious about how you could lend your personal experience and insight, this guide explains what this commitment entails and put you on the right path to becoming an effective mentor.

As a mentee, mentor and founder of Dream Girls Mentoring Program, Tamira Dunn demonstrates how the role of a mentor is an integral part of maximizing one's full potential by teaching new skills and techniques to accelerate success paths. You'll gain the necessary clarity regarding:

• Who to mentor

• What to do during troubling instances

• When to summon additional support

• Where to volunteer

• How to establish a trusting relationship

• Why you want to be a mentor

Don't let your achievements end with you. BE P.R.O.U.D teaches you how to build a legacy that will last long after you are done reading.
