Love...Light & Life!!! What is it all about? Three words can mean so much, but in themselves are just three little words. Just like the three little words 'I Love You!!!' The poetry and philosophy contained within this book contains just that, words, some large some small, but all very meaningful. There is a poem in this book for everyone. Happy poems, sad poems, poems for lovers and also for those who feel unloved. You will also find contained within; my philosophies, that although may not be the complete 'Meaning of Life,' will at least give everybody a pause for positive thought. We all love to be in LOVE!!! We are all searching for the LIGHT!!! But are we are all living our LIFE to its fullest? I truly hope my book helps you on your way, towards the enlightenment in this life you are searching for. I wish you well on your journey. David Michael
